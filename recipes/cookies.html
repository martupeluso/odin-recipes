Rapunzel's Chocolate Cookies

Description

Ready to have the "BEST. DAY. EVER!"? Make it happen with this delicious Chocolate Cookies recipe!

These chocolate cookies are adventurous and sweet, just like our favorite princess 🥰

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 3/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1/3 cup coconut oil
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened applesauce
  • 1 cup chocolate chips
  • 2/3 cup maple syrup
  • Skillet

Steps

  1. First, whisk together flour, baking soda, salt and nutmeg in a bowl.
  2. Next you’ll add the coconut oil, maple syrup and the apple sauce together in a bowl stirring.
  3. Once the liquid mixture is stirred well, pour it into the flour mixture and add in the chocolate chips.
  4. Pour the dough into a frying pan and bake the cookie dough for 20-25 minutes at 350 degrees.
  5. Test the cookie with a fork in the middle to make sure the dough is cooked well. The cookie should end up being a nice golden brown!
